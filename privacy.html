Exodus From Darkness

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Exodus From Darkness (“EFD,” “we,” “our”) built this app to help people grow in faith through Bible reading, teaching, media, and ministry resources. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum.

Overview

This policy describes how the Exodus From Darkness iOS app handles information. It applies to the mobile app published by Wilde Productions on behalf of Exodus From Darkness Inc.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store some preferences locally on your iPhone or iPad, including:

  • Your optional first name (for personalized greetings)
  • Bible reading position, bookmarks, and highlights
  • Appearance settings (such as light or dark mode)
  • Onboarding and splash screen preferences
  • Cached articles, media listings, and ministry content for faster loading
  • Prayer journey progress and “I Prayed” responses

This information stays on your device unless you choose to share content (for example, sharing a verse image).

Information You Choose to Submit

If you sign up for the EFD newsletter inside the app, we collect the first name, last name, and email address you enter. That information is sent securely to our newsletter provider (Mailchimp) through an automated integration so we can send you ministry updates.

If you donate, shop, or complete other forms on exodusfromdarkness.org, those actions happen in your web browser and are handled under that website’s practices.

How We Use Information

On-device data is used only to improve your experience in the app — for example, remembering where you left off in Scripture or saving verses you bookmark.

Newsletter information is used only to manage your subscription and send ministry communications you requested.

We do not sell your personal information.

Photo Library

If you choose to save a shared Bible verse image, the app may ask permission to add that image to your photo library. We do not access or upload your other photos.

Third-Party Services

The app may load content from EFD and trusted third-party services, including:

  • Sanity CMS (articles, ministry updates, prayer requests, course updates)
  • Bible text API (bible.helloao.org)
  • YouTube and podcast feeds for teaching media
  • Mailchimp (newsletter signups submitted from the app)
  • Exodus From Darkness website links (donate, store, contact, books, and course pages)

When you open external links, you leave the app and those services have their own privacy practices.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics SDKs to profile you across other companies’ apps or websites.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app grows. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
